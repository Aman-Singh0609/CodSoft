CLOUD COMPUTING AND VIRTUALIZATION
UPCOMING CHANGE

Cloud computing is transforming the technology landscape by offering scalable, on-demand access to computing resources, which significantly boosts business agility and operational efficiency. By minimizing the reliance on physical hardware and facilitating remote work, cloud computing streamlines IT management and drives cost savings. Advances in AI and machine learning within cloud environments are propelling innovation in data analysis, automation, and intelligent decision-making. As a cornerstone of digital transformation, cloud computing is poised to revolutionize industries, enhance cybersecurity, and create a more interconnected and intelligent global ecosystem.
